The following materials are needed when an individual is applying for ICP filing:

> **Note:**
> > Only electronic copies (scans or photos) are needed for the following materials.

## Personal Identity Document (Required)

| Identity |Required document|
| -------- | ---------------------- |
| Chinese citizen | Identity card |
| Hong Kong and Macao resident | Mainland travel permit for Hong Kong and Macao residents |
| Taiwan resident | Mainland travel permit for Taiwan residents |
| Foreigner | Passport |

## Website ICP Filing Information Authenticity Checklist (Required)
This is automatically generated by the system during the application process.
> You won't need to submit Website ICP Filing Information Authenticity Checklist if you apply via Mini Program. You only need **Letter of Undertaking for Website ICP Filing Application**, which will be automatically generated during the application process.

## Other Materials

| Item | Note |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Domain name certificate | In Hunan, Guizhou, Jiangsu, Shaanxi, Shandong (if more than 4 domain names are submitted at a time) and Henan (if more than 10 domain names are submitted at a time), individual applicant should provide domain name certificate. |
| Temporary residence permit or residence permit | In Sichuan, Shanghai, Jiangsu, Jiangxi and Hubei, if the address on the individual applicant's identity card is in another province, temporary residence permit or residence permit should be submitted. |
| Website construction proposal | In Guangdong, if the individual applicant has already obtained ICP filing for more than 10 websites, a website construction proposal should be submitted when applying for ICP filing for new websites. |
