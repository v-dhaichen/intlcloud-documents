> Only electronic copies (scans or photos) are needed for the following materials.

## Materials for personal ICP filing application


### Personal identity document (required)

| Identity | Document required |
| -------- | ---------------------- |
| Mainland China resident | Identity card |
| Hong Kong and Macao resident | Mainland travel permit for Hong Kong and Macao residents |
| Taiwan resident | Mainland travel permit for Taiwan residents |
| Foreigner | Passport |


### Other materials

| Item | Note |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Domain name certificate | In Inner Mongolia, Hunan, Jiangsu, Guizhou, Chongqing, Shaanxi (if 5 or more domain names are submitted at a time), and Henan (if 5 or more domain names are submitted at a time), individual applicant shall provide domain name certificates. |
| Temporary residence permit or residence permit | In Sichuan, Shanghai, Jiangsu, Jiangxi and Hubei, if the address on the individual applicant's identity card is in another province, temporary residence permit or residence permit shall be submitted. |
| Website construction proposal | If an applicant has already obtained ICP filing for more than 4 websites (in general), a **website construction proposal** shall be submitted. |


## Materials for organizational ICP filing application
### Organizational identity document (required)

| Organization type | Document required (choose one) |
| ---------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Government agency | Organization code certificate, certificate of unified social credit code |
| Public institution | Organization code certificate, legal person certificate of public institution |
| Enterprise | Business license (certificate of unified social credit code) |
| Social organization | Legal person registration certificate of social organization, organization code certificate |
| Private non-enterprise entity | Organization code certificate, registration certificate of private non-enterprise entity |
| Foundation | Organization code certificate, legal person registration certificate of foundation |
| Law firm | Organization code certificate, practicing license of law firm |

### Personal identity document of the person-in-charge (required)

| Identity | Document required |
| -------- | ---------------------- |
| Mainland China resident | Identity card |
| Hong Kong and Macao resident | Mainland travel permit for Hong Kong and Macao residents |
| Taiwan resident | Mainland travel permit for Taiwan residents |
| Foreigner | Passport |


### Other materials

| Item | Note |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Domain name certificate | In Inner Mongolia, Hunan, Jiangsu, Guizhou, Chongqing, Shaanxi (if 5 or more domain names are submitted at a time), and Henan (if 5 or more domain names are submitted at a time), individual applicant shall provide domain name certificate. |
| Prerequisite or special approval | If the contents of the website involve news, publications, medicine and medical devices, radio, film and television programs, or cultural activities, the prerequisite or special approvals issued by applicable authorities should be submitted. If the website contains medical-related contents, please consult the competent provincial food and drug administration to determine whether any prerequisite approvals are required. For more information, see [Prerequisite Approvals](https://intl.cloud.tencent.com/document/product/1039/32931). |
| Legal person's power of attorney for person-in-charge of entity | In Henan, Sichuan, and Gansu, if the person-in-charge of entity filled in is not the legal person, **legal person's power of attorney for person-in-charge of entity** shall be submitted. |
| Legal person's power of attorney for person-in-charge of website | In Hunan, Hubei and Tianjin, if the person-in-charge of website filled in is not the legal person, **legal person's power of attorney for person-in-charge of website** shall be submitted. |
| Website construction proposal | If an applicant has already obtained ICP filing for more than 4 websites (in general), a **website construction proposal** shall be submitted. |
